how it’s done

BEATphx is a simple browser beat machine. Pick a bank, tap the six pads, record a loop, then export your beat to your phone files.

1. Pick sounds
Use the bank buttons. Drum banks, bass, chords, and synths can all be layered without changing the sounds you already recorded.
2. Record a loop
Tap REC. It waits on standby until your first pad hit. After the loop starts, tap REC to turn overdub on or off while the loop keeps playing.
3. Build while it plays
While the loop is playing, tap Record again to overdub more sounds. Quantize can be turned on or off.
4. Sample your mic
Turn on the mic, choose a pad, then record. You can rap, sing, beatbox, or capture a small song snippet. Use the silent 3 to 0 countdown or instant record.

Use Predetermined Measures for clean 1 to 8 bar loops, or Freeform Loop to capture the exact start and stop timing of your performance. Use pad effects for the pad you are playing. Use master effects for the whole mix. Name your file before saving. MP3 export uses an online encoder when available. If the encoder is blocked, the app saves a WAV backup.
