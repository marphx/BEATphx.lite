how it's done

★ READ THIS BEFORE YOU COOK ★
  1. Pick a bank up top. The six pads change by bank, and sampled pads stay locked to the bank and pad where you recorded them.
  2. Tap REC once to arm standby. Your first pad hit starts the loop. Tap REC again to close the first pass. Loops can go up to 60 seconds.
  3. Tap PLAY to stop or restart the loop. Tap REC while the loop is running to overdub more sounds.
  4. QUANT is a toggle. When it is on, new hits snap tighter. When it is off, your timing stays raw.
  5. Open the PERFORMANCE and SAMPLER windows when you need controls. Hide them if you want a cleaner beat machine view.
  6. The sampler lets you record your voice, rap, sing, beatbox, or grab a snippet into a pad. You can choose whether the silent countdown is on or off.
  7. Use UNDO and REDO to move through loop edits. Use SAVE to export your beat as a WAV file.
